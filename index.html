Leading the industry for over 38 years

AGRIMOT has been at the forefront of electrical equipment distribution for over 38 years. We provide a wide range of high-quality electrical solutions trusted by contractors, builders, and industrial clients across the nation. Our focus remains on delivering reliable products and exceptional service that meet the evolving needs of modern electrical systems. As an authorised channel partner of Lauritz Knudsen, we bring globally trusted quality and advanced electrical solutions to our customers.

Our product portfolio includes ACB, MCCB, switch disconnector, change over switch, MCB, isolator, RCCB, RCBO, SPD, fuse link & fuse carrier, DB, power contactors & relays, indicator, push button, motor starters, IP65 enclosure, FRP meter and junction box and a complete range of electrical protection, automaton and power management solutions. With an unwavering commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction, we have become a preferred partner for thousands of projects—from residential developments to major industrial installations.
